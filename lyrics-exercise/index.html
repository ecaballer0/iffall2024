Wednesday is an indie band from Asheville North Carolina mainly inspire by the alt rock and shoegaze genres. The lead vocalist, Karly Hartzman, writes most of the songs the band performs and is accompanied by MJ Lederman on guitar, Xandy Chelmas on lap steel, Alan Miller on drums, and Ethan Baechtold on bass. The band is currently on break after touring their last album "Rat Saw God" while member MJ Lenderman tours his most recent album release: Manning Fireworks.
